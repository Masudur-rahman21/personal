Muhammad Nahidur Rahman (Professionally known as Sarfaraz Naheed) is a nasheed & acapella artist, sound engineer and entrepreneur. In 20 May 1999 (20/05/1999) he was born in Bangladesh. Sarfaraz Naheed is the founder and CEO of "Azmaeen Records". It is a Record label company. Lots of Islamic music are published through his label. This is a successful music label company. He is also founder of "Al-Araf Digital". It is an IT organization.
